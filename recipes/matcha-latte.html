My Daily Matcha Latte

Description

This is the matcha latte that I made every day. I am not even over exaggerating, it's my breakfast. I used to be a barista and ever since, I cannot be stopped.

I have even gotten to the point of buying my own ingredients to make it at home now. After trying different matcha powders and milk brands, I have now created the perfect (basic) matcha latte.

Ingredients

  • a little water like ~1/4 of your glass
  • matcha powder - pick your favorite! I like this one it's quick to dissolve and I am impatient
  • milk of choice I use this one (thanks starbucks)
  • ice iced drinks forever ðŸ©·

Directions

  1. two level-ish scoops of matcha powder
  2. add it to the water and mix until dissolved
  3. add in ice
  4. add milk
  5. stir until combined
  6. enjoy!
